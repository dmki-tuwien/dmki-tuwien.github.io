Tools and Results

How is RDF validation really used in practice?

Between December 2024 and March 2025, we asked the global RDF community exactly that. The result is a rich snapshot of today’s validation landscape — covering tools, workflows, challenges, and unmet needs across academia and industry.

Beyond publishing our findings, we go one step further: we open up the survey itself. Through an interactive website, you can explore the raw data, reproduce our analyses, and uncover your own insights. Dive in, query the data, and see how your experiences compare to the broader community.
