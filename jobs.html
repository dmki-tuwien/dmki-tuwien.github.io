MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships 2026

We are looking for outstanding postdoctoral researchers to join our team at TU Wien. The DMKI Lab conducts research at the intersection of data management, knowledge graphs, and AI. If you are interested in these topics and joining our efforts, we are happy to support your application for an MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowship.

Important Information
  • Call: MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships 2026 (HORIZON-MSCA-2026-PF-01)
  • Opening date: April 9, 2026
  • Final Deadline: September 9, 2026
  • Application deadline: Rolling (applications are reviewed as received)

Interested candidates should send a notice of interest along with a draft of their application materials to Prof. Katja Hose.
