Open Positions

PhD and Postdoc positions

If you're interested in joining the lab - as PhD student or postdoctoral researcher - but don't see an open position that fits, feel free to get in touch with the following information:

  • Cover letter (max. 1 page), including (i) motivation for applying, (ii) preferred starting date, and (iii) a brief explanation of the applicant's background
  • CV
  • List of publications if available
  • Recommendation letters or contact information of at least one reference, e.g., master thesis supervisor
  • For PhD candidates: a copy of the master thesis if already available or short summary otherwise
  • Copies of certificates incl. grade sheet or transcript
  • Application deadline: Rolling (applications are reviewed as received)

MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships 2025

We are looking for outstanding postdoctoral researchers to join our team at TU Wien. The DMKI Lab conducts research at the intersection of data management, knowledge graphs, and AI. If you are interested in these topics and joining our efforts, we are happy to support your application for an MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowship.

Important Information
  • Call: MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships 2025 (HORIZON-MSCA-2025-PF)
  • Final Deadline: September 10, 2025
  • Application deadline: Rolling (applications are reviewed as received)

Interested candidates should send a notice of interest along with a draft of their application materials to Prof. Katja Hose.
