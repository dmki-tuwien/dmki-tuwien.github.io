Teaching

We regularly offer Bachelor's and Master's theses in the areas of data management and artificial intelligence. Our topics are often embedded in ongoing research projects, providing the opportunity to contribute to cutting-edge research.

Current Thesis Topics

  • Knowledge Graphs
  • Large Language Models
  • Improving the Quality of Knowledge Graphs
  • Synthetic and Evolving Knowledge Graphs
  • Provenance and Explainability
  • Data Integration and Sharing
  • Efficient Query Processing over Heterogeneous Data Sources

If you're interested, feel free to contact us by email. We're also happy to discuss your own topic proposals!
