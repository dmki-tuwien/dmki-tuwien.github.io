About Us

We are the Data Management and Knowledge-Driven AI (DMKI) laboratory. Our research lab is situated at the Institute of Logic and Computation at TU Wien in Vienna, Austria. Our research focuses on cutting-edge advancements in Knowledge Graphs, Data Management, AI, and their applications in healthcare, analytics, and more.
