About Us

The  Data Management and Knowledge-Driven AI (DMKI) Lab is part of the Databases and Artificial Intelligence (DBAI) group at the Institute of Logic and Computation of TU Wien's Faculty of Informatics in Vienna, Austria. 

Our research focuses on cutting-edge advancements in knowledge graphs, data management, AI, and their applications in healthcare, analytics, and more.
